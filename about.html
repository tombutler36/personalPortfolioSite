Who am I

I am originally from Seattle, WA where I attended University Preparatory Academy highschool from 2012-2016. Now, I am located in Los Angeles, CA where I am a senior at Occidental College and will graduate in May of 2021. I grew up immersed in amateur athletics playing basketball and baseball through highschool and continued to play and coach collegiate baseball at Occidental College. My hobbies include, cooking, biking, surfing and teaching myself new skills.

Education

I attend Occidental College in Los Angeles, CA where I am pursuing my Bachelor of Arts, double majoring in Mathematics and Kinesiology. I am a four year deans list appearant, and have been a two year kinesiology department ambassador.

Work Experience

I currently work as an Operations Research Intern for SportsID inc. a sports technology start-up based out of Atlanta, GA. I also have four years of experience in being a teachers assistant and private tutor, having worked closely with professors to deliver efficient learning environments in courses such as Linear Algebra, Partial Differential Equations, Human Anatomy and Biomechanics.

Athletic Career

Four year member (2016-2020) of the Occidental College varsity baseball team. Two year releif pitcher and two year starting pitcher, logging a career 115 innings pitched with a 3.67 ERA. Served as team captain for the 2019-2020 season and assistant pitching coach for the 2020-2021 season.
